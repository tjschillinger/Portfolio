Business Summary

Reggie is a raw food educator, providing in-person classes, coaching consulting, and online courses.

My Role

Lead UX Designer

Stylistic Themes

Fresh | Artistic | Free Spirit | Family Fun

Objectives

Primary

New Clientele via the booking tool.

Alt Primary

New Clientele via his Phone Call

Secondary Objective

New Clientele via the contact form on the "Contact" page.

User Experience

Stylistic Impressions

Reggie wanted his site to express the memories that come from enjoying food together in community. He wanted his site to make him look professional while free-spirited.

Prime Audience

Parents (mostly mothers) in their 30s looking to revamp the dietary habits of their families.

Reference Site

The Raw Cheif: The client enjoyed the layout and different types of sections.

Themes

General Impressions

Reggie wanted his users to feel at home visiting his site, while feeling like they were in the hands of a professional.

Imagery

Reggie requested imagery of family members having fun. For food imagery, he wanted at-home and professional cooking environments, especially with raw vegetables.

Colors

Reggie requested black, white, gold or orange, deep rich banana leaf green, and any colors associated with succulents and cacti.

Fonts

The client requested something easy to read, artistic, and personalized.

Distinctive Features

Reggie requested some image of Succulents and Cacti be placed on the "About" page, since it was apart of his branding.
