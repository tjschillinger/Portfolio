Raw with Reggy | Lifestyle Webiste

Business Summary

Reggie came to me for a website that would make him look professional as a raw food educator and bring in new clientele for in-person classes, coaching, consulting, and online courses.

My Role

UX/UI Designer

Stylistic Themes

Fresh | Nostalgic | Professional

Primary Audience

Parents (mostly mothers) in their 30s looking to revamp the dietary habits of their families.

Website Objective

Gain new clientele through the booking tool and the contact form on the Contact page.

REQUIREMENTS

Nostalgic Tones

To draw in his primary user (mothers), Reggie wanted his site to express the memories that come from enjoying food together.


Brand Building

Reggie wanted his site to make him look like an industry expert in both professional and home environments. He also wanted his site to look fresh and not too corporate. His brand colors included orange, deep green, and any colors associated with succulents. Succulents were associated with his branding, so he requested a bold image of them be on placed his About page.


Inspiration

The Raw Chef: Reggie sent this website as an example of the layout and tone he wanted his site to express.

PROCESS AND RESULTS

Nostalgic Tones

To meet this requirement, I chose to add a shadow and 10px border to the images to associate them with polaroid pictures. And for some sections, I skewed the image positions to give the effect of the images being spread across a table. Also, I chose to design sections with background images of wood that would draw associations with picnic tables.

Brand Building

To balance the professional and fresh aspect of Reggie's brand, I chose to provide clean sections and playful ones, alternating them down the layout. For the typography, I styled them in the professional, yet playfully fresh manner of having an easy-to-read font to begin with, then a colorful script at the end.
