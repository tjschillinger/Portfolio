Business Summary

Altered Reality helps their clientele create branded content and wedding memorabilia.

My Role

Lead UX/UI Designer

Stylistic Themes

Modern | Artistic

Objectives

Primary

New Clientele via their booking tool.

Alt Primary

New Clientele through their "Start Here" page (taking a quiz).

Secondary Objective

New Clientele via the contact form on their "Contact" page.

User Experience

Stylistic Impressions

Altered Reality wanted their site's content to be the star. They wanted to win over new clientele with their video testimonials and a professional look.

Prime Audience

Brides and grooms, and green businesses.

Reference Site

Magnum Digital Cinema: The client enjoyed the grid layout and how this site made their process stand out.

Themes

General Impressions

Altered Reality needed their process to stand out. Also, they wanted only the imagery to pop in terms of color. Everything else they wanted back or white.

Imagery

Altered Reality wanted their imagery to focus on beautiful nature shots and weddings. Bonus for nailing both.

Colors

Black and white. Color can only come from imagery and video content.

Fonts

Altered Reality requested a font that was easy to read and not too corporate looking. They're clientele tended to be younger and fun-seeking.

Distinctive Features of Altered Reality

They created content through the view point of storytelling. They services included "Brand Stories" and "Love Stories."
