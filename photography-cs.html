Altered Reality | Digital Services

Business Summary

Altered Reality came to me for a website that would spread brand awareness and gain new clientele for branded content and wedding memorabilia. They create content through the view point of storytelling. Their services include "Brand Stories" and "Love Stories."

My Role

UX/UI Designer

Stylistic Themes

Modern | Nature | Wedding

Prime Audience

Brides and grooms, and green businesses.

Website Objectives

Gain new clientele through the booking tool, the quiz on the Start Here page, and the contact form on the Contact page.

Requirements

Showcase Their Content

80% of Altered Reality's serives included content creation for their clientele. So they wanted their site's content to be the star.

Showcase Their Process

Most of Altered Reality's new inquiries were questions about their process for content creation, rather than solicitations for their serives. They wanted their process showcased in order to reduce the knowledge gap and streamline inquiries.

Brand Colors

Black and white.

Inspiration

Magnum Digital Cinema: Altered Reality sent this website as an example of the layout and tone they wanted their site to express.

Process And Results

Showcase Their Content

To make Altered Reality's content stand out, I decided to rely on darker sections and permit color to only come from imagery or Altered Reality's content. Since the primary user of Altered Reality's services are wedding and green business clientele, I decided to have some sections decorated with imagery that showcased natural landscapes and wedding events.

Showcase Their Process

To make Altered Reality's process stand out, I placed it on a section that had the background of an abstract, parallaxed image, giving it a modern feel. Then I decided to structure the steps in this format:

A) Icon -to anchor the user's attention.
B) H3 Title -to orientate the user to the information.
C) Description -to educate the user.

Typography

I chose the Asap font for Altered Reality's website because their clientele tended to be younger and fun-seeking.
