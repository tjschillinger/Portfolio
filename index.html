My Process

My story of applying a solution (approach to design).

Product's Scope

Deadlines, team size, and funding, all of these determine the environment of what can be accomplished.

User Research

Who's the user? Armed with interviews, surveys, and personas, I slash out a wanted, user-centered product.

User Stories

To create an emotional response, the product must realize a narrative. What story will this product tell?

Wireframing

To contextualize an experience, elements need an intuitive place to live and a hierarchy of information.

Prototyping

The interactions of the user need to be mimicked to see if all user flows are as seamless as predicted.

Final Iterations

A final round of user testing and iterations ensures that egos and biases haven't hyjacked the project.

About Me

My career in UX design stems from my relentless will to answer the question, what is the good life? This question has led me to thumb through the minds of philosophers, uproot myself from my home state, and ask people, "how does this site make you feel?" The accumulation of my pursuit has led me to UX design, because chipping away at the answer of what is the good life is the essence of good UX design. The good life is a story, so is good UX. The good life is social, so is good UX. The good life takes place in an established environment, so does good UX.
